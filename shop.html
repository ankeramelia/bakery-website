Bread for Better

At Hearth & Crumb, we believe that food has the power to bring people together and change lives. That’s why we started our Bread for Better initiative — a commitment to donating a portion of every loaf sold to support local shelters and community feeding programs.

For every artisan sourdough or golden baguette you enjoy, you’re helping us provide freshly baked bread to those who need it most. It’s our way of sharing the warmth of our hearth with the wider community, one loaf at a time.

Because at Hearth & Crumb, we believe that every crumb counts — not just in our baking, but in the difference we make together.
